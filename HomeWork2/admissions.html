Admissions

Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP) offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The admission process is competitive and aims to select the best candidates.

Undergraduate Admissions

The undergraduate admission process typically includes:

  • Application Form Submission
  • Admission Test
  • Interview

Postgraduate Admissions

The postgraduate admission process typically includes:

  • Application Form Submission
  • Written Test
  • Interview
